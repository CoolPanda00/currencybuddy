What are Currency Pairs?

Forex quotes always show currency pairs, e.g. EUR/USD. The first currency is the base, the second is the quote currency.

Major, Minor, Exotic Pairs

  • Majors: Most traded pairs with USD.
  • Minors: Pairs without USD but major economies.
  • Exotics: Major currency + emerging economy currency.
