What are Currency Pairs?

Forex quotes always show currency pairs, e.g. EUR/USD. The first currency is the base, the second is the quote currency.

Major, Minor, Exotic Pairs

  • Majors: Most traded pairs with USD.
  • Minors: Pairs without USD but major economies.
  • Exotics: Major currency + emerging economy currency.

Understanding Currency Pairs

In forex trading, currencies are quoted in pairs. The first currency is the base currency, and the second is the quote currency. The value shows how much of the quote currency is needed to buy one unit of the base currency.

Pair Description
EUR/USD Euro against US Dollar (most traded pair globally)
USD/JPY US Dollar against Japanese Yen
GBP/USD Pound Sterling against US Dollar
