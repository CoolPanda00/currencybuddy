How Global Events Affect Exchange Rates

Political Events & Instability

Elections, political instability, and policy changes create uncertainty, leading to volatility in currency values.

Event Impact Example
Brexit Vote GBP fell sharply against major currencies due to economic uncertainty.
US Elections USD experiences volatility around policy change expectations.

Natural Disasters & Pandemics

Events like earthquakes, tsunamis, or pandemics slow down economies and weaken currencies due to reduced productivity and growth.

Global Events & Forex

Global events like geopolitical tensions, pandemics, wars, and major trade agreements significantly influence forex markets by creating uncertainty or confidence, affecting currency demand.

Event Impact
War Decreases currency value due to instability
Trade Agreement Strengthens currency by boosting economic prospects
